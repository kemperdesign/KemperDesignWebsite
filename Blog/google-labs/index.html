What is Google Labs?

Google Labs is an experimental playground where Google’s engineers test "bold and responsible" AI ideas. It’s not just one tool; it’s a portfolio of experiments—from AI-powered notebooks to creative music generators. (Google Labs)

Being an early adopter isn't about being a "tech geek." It’s about operational leverage. If you can use a Labs experiment to automate a task while your competitors are still doing it manually, you win.
