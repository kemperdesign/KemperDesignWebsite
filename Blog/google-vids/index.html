The Video-First Workplace: Reimagining Communication with Google Vids

Let’s be honest: no one wants to read another 20-slide PowerPoint deck. In 2026, the businesses that communicate most effectively aren't just writing; they're "showing." Google Vids is the tool that turns your documents, charts, and ideas into professional video stories—without needing an editor. (Google Vids)

What is Google Vids?

Google Vids is an AI-powered video creation app for Google Workspace. It’s designed specifically for work communication—think team updates, training modules, and project pitches. You provide the content (Docs, Sheets, Slides), and the AI "stems" together a video with stock footage, transitions, and even a AI voiceover.

Why This Matters for Your Business

Engagement is the biggest hurdle for internal communication. If your team isn't reading your emails, they aren't following your strategy. At Kemper Design Services, we use Vids to help our clients create "snackable" video updates that their teams actually watch. It’s about being "video-first" without the high production cost.

Trending Tasks & Features

  • Automated Storyboarding: Having the AI draft the visual flow of your video based on your project brief.
  • AI Voiceover Integration: Instantly adding a professional narration to your project updates.
  • In-App Recording: Recording your screen or webcam directly inside the video timeline for personalized touches.
  • Cloud Collaboration: Working on a video timeline with your team just as easily as a Google Doc.

The ROI: Attention Efficiency

A 2-minute video can communicate more than a 20-minute meeting. By using Google Vids to streamline your communication, you're reclaiming your team's most valuable asset: their attention. It’s the ultimate way to ensure your business stays aligned and informed.
