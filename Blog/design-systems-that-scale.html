Design Systems That Scale

By Ava Mitchell August 25, 2025 7 min read

Design systems are more than just a library of UI components. They are the language that teams use to communicate, build, and scale products. In this article, we explore the core principles of building a scalable design system.

From tokenization of colors and typography to the documentation of accessibility standards, a well-oiled design system empowers developers and designers to move at the speed of thought without sacrificing consistency.

Why Scale Matters

As organizations grow, the number of screens and edge cases multiplies. A manual approach to design quickly becomes unmanageable. We'll look at how industry leaders use automated pipelines to sync design tokens from Figma directly into codebases.
