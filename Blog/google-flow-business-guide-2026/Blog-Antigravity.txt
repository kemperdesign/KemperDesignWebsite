Google Antigravity is a new AI-powered development platform designed around **autonomous AI agents that can plan, execute, and validate tasks with minimal human input**. ([Google Developers Blog][1])

Instead of simply assisting with tasks (like traditional AI tools), Antigravity allows businesses to **delegate entire workflows to AI**, including:

* Planning processes
* Executing tasks across systems
* Testing and validating outcomes
* Iterating automatically

This “agent-first” approach represents a major shift—AI is no longer just a helper; it becomes a **digital operator working alongside your business**. ([Scalable Path][2])
