Our Story

From Blueprints to Intelligent Systems

Built on decades of precision engineering, evolved into practical AI solutions that help businesses work smarter.

The Foundation

Kemper Design Services was built on precision long before automation existed. In the late 1980s, Mike Kemper began drawing architectural blueprints by hand—where every line had to be exact, and every detail mattered.

That early work required discipline, structure, and a deep understanding of how complex systems come together. Those same principles still define how we operate today.

What started as manual drafting evolved into digital design. What became digital systems evolved into automation. Today, we help businesses integrate AI into their everyday operations.
