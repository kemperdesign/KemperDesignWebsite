About Kemper Design Services

Kemper Design Services is a team of AI experts, developers, and strategists based in St. Augustine, FL, dedicated to helping small businesses and teams modernize their operations without the enterprise price tag. We work remotely with clients across the United States and internationally.

We believe AI should be practical, accessible, and designed to enhance human potential — not replace it. Whether it's automating workflows, building custom applications, or deploying intelligent systems, we create solutions that solve real business problems. Our mission is to help you recover the hours you've been losing to manual work, inefficient processes, and disconnected tools.

We are hands-on builders with a consulting mindset. We don't sell software licenses or send reports — we build things and stay through implementation. 90% of our clients return for a second project, and return clients get priority scheduling.

Our AI & Business Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of AI automation, design, training, and technology services for small and mid-size businesses. Every engagement is scoped to your exact needs with a fixed price agreed in writing before work begins.
